## 1. 画面解析

参考博客首页背景图是一张宽幅暗色幻想插画。

核心元素：

- 超宽横构图，适合作为网页 hero 背景
- 低饱和灰蓝色调
- 浓重雾气、烟尘、战场废墟
- 中央近景是一个背对观众的小型战士/少女角色
- 角色手持长剑，站在画面底部中心
- 背景有三台巨型机械守卫或古代构装体
- 机械胸口和头部有橙黄色发光核心
- 画面中有碎石、火星、破损结构
- 整体氛围压迫、宏大、史诗感强
- 中央人物与巨型机械形成强烈尺度对比

视觉关键词：

```text
dark fantasy
giant ancient machines
misty battlefield
small hero silhouette
cinematic wide composition
desaturated blue gray
orange glowing cores
epic scale
ruins and smoke
```

## 5. 如果用于“纸间旅栈”

如果要贴合 `纸间旅栈`，不建议完全复刻战斗机甲图。可以保留它的构图和氛围，但把主题换成“纸页、旅栈、巨大知识构装体”：

```text
A cinematic ultra-wide dark fantasy illustration for a personal knowledge blog named "Paper Inn Between Pages", a lone traveler-scholar seen from behind standing at the entrance of a small paper-and-wood inn, facing three colossal archive golems made of folded paper, ink-stained wood, and ancient brass mechanisms, glowing warm amber cores, misty night, drifting paper sheets, broken stone path, distant mountains, desaturated blue-gray atmosphere with warm lantern highlights, epic scale contrast, quiet and mysterious, painterly concept art, no text, no logo, no watermark, 21:9 aspect ratio.
```
